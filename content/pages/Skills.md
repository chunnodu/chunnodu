<h1>Top Talents</h1>

<h2>1. Decision Making</h2>
<p><strong>Definition:</strong> The ability to make high-quality decisions based on limited information.</p>
<h3>Key Strengths:</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Extraordinary</strong> at critically evaluating information, generating solutions, and using facts and logic to support decisions.</li>
    <li><strong>Excellent</strong> at accepting feedback and taking responsibility for decisions.</li>
    <li><strong>Very capable</strong> of anticipating problems and choosing forward-thinking solutions.</li>
</ul>

<h2>2. Innovation</h2>
<p><strong>Definition:</strong> The ability to generate novel solutions and creative ideas to solve problems.</p>
<h3>Key Strengths:</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Extraordinary</strong> at working with abstract ideas and developing unconventional solutions.</li>
    <li><strong>Very good</strong> at understanding complicated problems, generating solutions, and evaluating options.</li>
</ul>

<h2>3. Persuasion</h2>
<p><strong>Definition:</strong> The ability to convince others of a direction, activity or idea, and influence decision-making.</p>
<h3>Key Strengths:</h3>
<ul>
    <li><strong>Excellent</strong> at understanding peoples' motivations and feelings, and knowing how to motivate others to change.</li>
    <li><strong>Capable</strong> of swaying the thinking and behavior of individuals who initially disagree.</li>
</ul>
